Janhavi's Bio

Janhavi Giri has been in love with words since her school days. She first discovered the magic of writing in her 8th grade — when simple poems and short articles became her way of capturing thoughts the heart could feel but the voice couldn’t always say.

What began as scribbles in notebooks slowly evolved into a deeper creative calling. In 2024, Janhavi took a leap into the professional writing world through her page Vichar Ativichar, a platform where she shares her own thoughts, collaborates with authors and publishers, and connects with readers who find a piece of themselves in her words.

Her style is rooted in real-life moments — sometimes raw, sometimes gentle — but always honest. She writes about situations we all live through: love, loss, hope, resilience, and the quiet truths we often overlook.

A B.Tech graduate and a working professional, Janhavi proves that passion doesn’t have to wait for the “right time” — it grows alongside every chapter of life. She currently lives in Mumbai, where the city’s pace fuels her dreams, while her hometown Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra, remains her anchor and inspiration.

When she’s not writing, you might find her exploring new books, having long conversations about life, or simply observing the world around her — collecting moments that may one day turn into words.

"For me, writing is not just about telling a story. It’s about holding a mirror to life and letting someone, somewhere, say — this is exactly how I feel."
— Janhavi Giri
